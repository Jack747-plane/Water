How to Save Water Reliably

Saving water is important every day. If everyone uses water carefully, we can protect clean water for the future.

Simple Daily Actions

These are easy habits that everyone can follow.

Short Showers

Taking short showers helps reduce water use.

Turn Off Taps

Turn off the tap while brushing teeth or washing hands.

Reuse Water

Reuse water for cleaning plants or floors.

Saving Water at Home

Small changes at home can save a lot of water.

Fix Leaks

Leaking taps waste water and should be repaired quickly.

Full Washing Loads

Only run washing machines when they are full.

Collect Rainwater

Rainwater can be used for cleaning and plants.

At School and in the Community

Working together helps protect water resources.

Learn and Share

Teach others about saving water.

Clean Areas

Keep rivers and streets clean from rubbish.

Awareness

Share information about water conservation.
