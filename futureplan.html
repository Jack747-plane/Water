Future Plan

Our goal is to make Yangon a cleaner, safer, and better place to live. We want to improve water, reduce pollution, and help people learn how to protect the environment.

Our Project Idea

This project is about improving water and the environment in Yangon. We want to stop pollution, save clean water, and help people understand how important water is.

Clean Water

Make sure people have safe drinking water by cleaning rivers and fixing dirty water systems.

Save Water

Teach people to turn off taps, fix leaks, and use only the water they need.

Reduce Trash

Stop plastic and rubbish from going into rivers and lakes by improving waste bins and recycling.

Community Help

Everyone in the community can help make the environment better.

School Programs

Teach students about saving water and keeping the environment clean.

Clean-Up Days

Organize group clean-up activities in parks, rivers, and streets.

Awareness

Use posters and campaigns to tell people why water protection is important.

Using Technology

Technology can help us solve water problems faster and better.

Water Sensors

Sensors can check if water is clean or dirty in real time.

Leak Detection

Technology can find water leaks in pipes so we don’t waste water.

Tracking Usage

We can track how much water people use and help reduce waste.

What Will Improve

These changes will make life better for everyone in Yangon.

Cleaner Environment

Less pollution in rivers, lakes, and streets.

Healthier People

Clean water means fewer diseases and better health.

Better City

Yangon will become a cleaner, greener, and more modern city.

Elemental Guardians

Building awareness for sustainable water systems and a smarter future for Yangon.
