Our Team

We are a group of four students known as The Elemental Guardians of Earth. Our team created this project to share information about Yangon's water systems and the importance of sustainable living. Through this website, we are going to share useful information about water resources, water systems, and smart solutions that can help reduce water waste throughout the community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to encourage citizens to conserve water through simple daily actions and smarter water management. We hope to inspire people to become more environmentally responsible while supporting the development of a hopefully cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable city for the future.

Why This Matters

Cause Consequence
Wasting water in daily activities Less clean water available for other homes and communities
Leaking pipes and old water systems Large amounts of water can be lost before reaching people
Throwing trash and chemicals into rivers or drains It can cause water pollution and unsafe water quality
Climate change and long dry seasons It could cause water shortages and can reduce the amount of water that is stored.
